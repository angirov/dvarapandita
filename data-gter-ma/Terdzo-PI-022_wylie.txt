phud skong 'gres byang /
dpal gyi gtor ma rgyan ldan g.yos su sbyar pa gser skyems kyi dam tshig gis bran pa'i phud kyi mchod pa dam pa 'dis gsol zhing mchod do //
sku gsung thugs kyi rgyud mnyes pa dang longs spyod kyi rkyen du 'byor bar gyur cig
/mthu dang rdzu 'phrul bsam gyis mi khyab pas thugs dam gnyan po las ma 'da' bar /
sangs rgyas kyi bstan pa srungs /
dkon mchog gi dbu 'phang bstod /
dge 'dun dang chos kyi rgyal srid skyongs /
snod kyi dge bcu sgrubs /
bcud kyi phan bde spel /
gdug pa can gyi gnya' gnon /
dam tshig can gyi stongs grogs mdzod /
thar 'dod kyi re ba skongs /
phan sems can gyi phrin las sgrubs /
khyad par bdag cag dpon slob yon mchod 'khor dang bcas pa rnams la sdang ba'i dgra thul /
gnod pa'i bgegs dpung choms /
nad dang nyer 'tshe sol /
tshe dang bsod nams spel /
dpal dang grags pa sgrubs /
dbang phyug dang gzi brjid skyed /
chos nor dang longs spyod spel /
mnga' ris dang chos srid skyongs /
dngos grub kyi bsam pa sgrubs /
phrin las kyi re ba skongs /
spyi dang khyad par gyi bsam pa thams cad yid bzhin du sgrub pa'i phrin las mdzad du gsol /
